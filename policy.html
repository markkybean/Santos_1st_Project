The National Security Policy 2023-2028 is the Philippines' overarching policy document that spells out the aspirations and defines the strategic direction of the whole government to protect, preserve, and enhance our national security. As partner document to the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028, the NSP provides guidance in the crafting of subsequent strategies and programs geared towards national security to build a conducive environment for development.
